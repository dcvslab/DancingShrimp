1. using my music in a youtube video 
So you want to use my music in a YouTube video? Sounds great! Here are some quick rules that I request you follow.

1.  You must get my music legally. Most of my music is free to download anyways so this shouldn't be a problem.

2. Do not say my music is your own, because it isn't.

3. Do not sell my music.

4. Do not make your own downloads(s) for my music, use my download(s) only.

5.  You cannot reupload my music to YouTube without adding your own creative content as this is promoting.

6. Please put at least the following in the description of the video, you may put more if you want.
-
Music by Shark EDM
http://youtube.com/sharkedm | http://sharkedm.com
Song(s) used: songname(s) go here
Song name: http://youtube.com/song-used
 http://sharkedm.com/song-used
-
If you used more than 1 song, repeat lines 3 and 4 until you include all the songs used.
The http://sharkedm.com/song-used link can be found can be found in the description of the YouTube video for it.


Example:
Music by Shark EDM
http://youtube.com/sharkedm | http://sharkedm.com
Songs used: Song1 and Song2
Song 1: http://youtube.com/song1
http://sharkedm.com/song1
Song 2: http://youtube.com/song2
http://sharkedm.com/song2


That is all for YouTube. Please remember that this can change at any time!

If you do not comply to my requests, I will take action.
