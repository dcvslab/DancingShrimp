DTEBOT COMMANDS

USER COMMANDS

dc | eta | fb | givespot | help | op | ping | rules

STAFF COMMANDS

noskip | reload | kill | meh

USER COMMANDS

dc | !dc

If you disconnected, this will move you back to you're spot.

eta | !eta

This will tell you the estimated time you have to wait to DJ.

fb | !fb

This will put the DTE FaceBook page into the chat.

givespot | !givespot @user

This will give your current waitlist posistion to a user. The user must be lower than you on the waitlist.

help | !help

This will put this website into chat.

op | !op

This will put the OP list into chat.

ping | !ping

Pong!

rules | !rules

This will put the rules in chat.

STAFF COMMANDS

noskip | !noskip |

This will make it so the next song will not be skipped.

reload | !reload |

This will reload the bot

kill | !kill |

This will kill the bot

meh | !meh # |

This will set the amount of mehs until a song is automatically skipped
